# The Status of Epopeia

This is our latest report on how we see ourselves. Updated on July 2026.

You can also see previous reports in our [history](/status/history).

## As of July 2026

We are still on-track!

### Systemically

Last year we worked on the [Foundations](/about) of the project and published them on this website, all while creating the first ties with the [target region](/status/location).

This year 2026 we have gone deeper, identifying the [Three Pillars](/about/the-three-pillars) of the project, short to remember, communicate, and orientate us.

We have as well identified and moved several ideas for businesses that we feel inspired to build, so many that we want to filter them.

We are participating again at the [European GEN Gathering](https://ecovillagegathering.org/) and strengthening links with potential partners of this central scene.

### Socially

The beginnings are being pretty shattered for us, Current Founders. Epopeia is still in a Nomadic Phase, which should not surprise anyone, but we forgot to account for that.

Although we were planning to move to the [Target Location](/status/location) in last autumn, we ended up exploring the possibility of joining forces with the [Circle Way Tribe](https://www.circlewaytribe.com/){:target="_blank"}. We spent energy and time together and learned to love each other. But we didn't continue that path as clarity arose.

Instead, we are temporarily located and co-living with a small community close to Koblenz (Germany). There we enjoy our first experience of being in a tighter communitarian field and with enough financial stability that we can work further on what the project needs next.

### Status, personally

A year ago we as [project partners](/status/team) met and moved together by means of fulfilling this project. We have since created the strongest interpersonal ties as primary bonding partners and resonate with each other wonderfully. We have advanced in personal growth, from _let's do it_ to _we're doing it_.

We have grown the community to a third member! His name is Imli. For the next time, he's got the Role of Guardian of Basic Needs and also of Teacher of Loving Attention. Many times he is also a Gifter of Joy for the community and friends. We are very fond of him already, even when he's just a toddler.

So we have also created the first Family of Epopeia. It includes a teenie, so it's pretty exciting. And it's challenging! Those are reasons for stronger prioritization in our daily lives.

We are full of ideas and dreams that want to be put in action.

We have finally come up with relevant next-step questions, e.g.: How is the process of joining Epopeia/the Founding Team? What [profile](/particupate/profile) do we wish from our next Founding Team members? How to properly co-create on a practical level, specially when there is reduced openness in the space?
